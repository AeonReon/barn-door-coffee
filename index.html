Our Story

Born From a Love of Proper Coffee & Real Baking

Founded by Hannah Pilkiewicz, Barn Door Coffee & Kitchen started with a simple idea — bring the North Coast a place where every coffee is speciality-grade and every bake is made from scratch, right here in Coleraine.

What began as one cosy cafe on Stone Row has grown into two locations, a mobile caravan for events, and a wholesale bakery supplying over 20 businesses across Northern Ireland.

Everything is made fresh using local, seasonal produce. No shortcuts. No mass-produced anything. Just proper food, proper coffee, and a warm welcome — whether you've got the kids, the dog, or just need five minutes of peace with a flat white and a brownie.
